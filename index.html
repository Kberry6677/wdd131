Pea Ridge, Arkansas

Pea Ridge, Arkansas is a small town in the Ozark Mountains of Northwest Arkansas. It's perhaps most famous as a site of an American civil war battle, The Battle of Pea Ridge. The site of the battle is preserved as the Pea Ridge National Military Park.
